Meet the Crucible

Blood and Thunder is a project developed by 23 students from the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC), currently in their third year of the Video Game Design and Development degree under the name of Crucible. This project lasted 4 and a half months and the objective was to gain the experience and knowledge of being able to work as a company and develop the game with our own engine and totally original.
