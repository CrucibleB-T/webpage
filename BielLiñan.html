I am Biel Liñán, a student at CITM with my main interest in game design. Throughout this project I centered mainly around level design and QA/UX.

About me

Since I can remember I loved building societies, stories and scenarios for different stories and now I would like to develop such scenarios into enjoyable experiences. I have always appreciated narrative driven experiences, I really enjoy reading books that make me feel invested in the story, I would like to transmit that to players.
