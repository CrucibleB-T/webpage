About me

I've been playing video games for as long as I can remember and they have always been one of my passions. I remember playing great titles like The Last of Us or the Uncharted saga and getting excited with their story and adoring their scenarios. In high school I entered the technological option with the intention of studying video games and once in the career I discovered 3D modelling and I knew what I wanted to dedicate myself to. I wanted to make people enjoy beautiful landscapes as I had enjoyed them as a child and I still enjoy them today. In short, I want to give back to videogames everything they have given me.
