I’m Sergi Gantzer Soler, a 23 year old studying Video Game Design and Development at CITM Barcelona. I’m an aspiring 3D Artist looking to find my way into the videogame industry.

About me

I’ve always found video games a form of art. A great way to escape reality, connect with friends and family and overall have a good time. Video games have helped me a lot throughout my life and this is my way to return the favor. By becoming a part of the industry and creating 3D art for videogames, I can be a part of what I love and hopefully help people throughout their lives.
