About me

My love for videogames comes from such an early age that I can't even remember a time in my life when they didn't exist.

I was lucky enough to have a father who played videogames long before I was born and that's largely where my passion for them comes from, since I was very young I have experienced both the view of the spectator and the player, and the more time I invested in them the more my passion for them grew.

And that is why today I want to work in this industry that has left me so many good memories in my childhood and I would love to be able to bring with my work to all future players half as many good memories as the game developers of my childhood have generated for me
