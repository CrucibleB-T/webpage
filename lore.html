Dieno

In the ashes of forgotten worlds, the legend of the Dieno is whispered among the battle-scarred. Forged in the crucible of endless war, their wings span as wide as the cosmos themselves, carrying the ancient wisdom of the Imperium’s earliest dawn.

Born from the melding of gene-engineering and xenotech, the Dieno soar above the battlefield as watchers and guardians. Tales tell of their ethereal cries echoing across the void, heralding both doom and redemption.

Sacrarium Mortis

The Space Marines (SM) stand as humanity’s ultimate defenders, clad in power armor and carrying weapons of righteous fury. Legends say that the “Blackwing Chapter” of SM traces its descent from a lost Primarch, its warriors bearing tattoos of winged skulls.
