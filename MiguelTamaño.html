Hello! I’m Miguel Tamaño Garon and I’m a student at CITM studying Game Design & Development. I’m really passionate about building video games and making the art for it. I feel very lucky to be part of this wonderful project and work alongside talented people to bring this game idea to life.

About me

I started to play video games when I was little, playing on a Game Boy. Since then, my love for video games has only grown and today I play all kinds of games in different platforms and genres. What makes me interested in video games are not only just gameplay, but everything behind it: the creativity, the design, the story, code, and especially the art. It always has been so fascinating to see little details that make a game come to life, also I have followed the industry closely for years, staying up to date with the latest games, innovations, trends and releases.

Originally, I was planning to become an aeronautical engineer and a pilot. But over time, I realized that I was as passionate in the world of game development. To me video games are more than entertainment, they are a powerful medium of expression. Whether it is through mechanics, the design or visual storytelling, games are just a perfect combination of technology and art. This exact combination is what excites me the most and that is why I chose this path.

Now I am proud to say that I’m studying what I love and working on meaningful projects with talented people. Game Development feels so challenging, expressive and incredibly rewarding for me.
