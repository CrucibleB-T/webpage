My name is Pol Celaya, and as a student at the Center for Image and Multimedia Technology - Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (CITM-UPC) in Barcelona, Spain.

About me

In my life I have two great hobbies, one is sports and the other is stories.

I have always enjoyed telling stories together and that is why I became fond of, or rather fell in love with, role-playing games at a very young age. Today, years later, I keep the habit of playing with my friends on a constant basis. Now I aspire to move to the next step and try to get to tell the stories I have inside me to a much wider audience thanks to the great loudspeaker that video games are.

Sportingly, I am an active Rugby player, a fantastic sport based on effort, sacrifice and family. Apart from playing, I am also an official educator of the federation who is allowed to coach children. In this experience as a mentor at C.N. Poblenou, my kids have taught me much more about myself than I could ever learn on my own
